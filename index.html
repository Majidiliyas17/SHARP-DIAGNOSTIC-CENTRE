Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How do I prepare for my blood test? +

Most blood tests require fasting for 8-12 hours. Drink water to stay hydrated. Avoid smoking and alcohol before the test. Continue prescribed medications unless your doctor advises otherwise.

How long does it take to get reports? +

Routine test reports are available within 24 hours. Specialized tests may take 2-3 days. Emergency tests can be expedited on request. All reports are delivered digitally via WhatsApp and email.

What areas do you serve for home collection? +

We provide home collection services across Lucknow including Alambagh, Aminabad, Aliganj, Gomti Nagar, Indira Nagar, Hazratganj, and surrounding areas. Please call to confirm service in your location.

Do you accept insurance? +

We are empaneled with major insurance providers and TPA companies. Please bring your insurance card and ID proof when visiting our center. For home collection, please check with your insurance provider about cashless facilities.
